Why This Shelter Matters

In Rochester, too many dogs and cats are suffering from abuse, neglect, and abandonment. They're not just strays — many of them have been thrown out, forgotten, or hurt by the people who were supposed to love them. These animals need help, and they deserve safety, healing, and love. That’s why I, Enora, am working to open a shelter: a place where these animals can recover, be cared for, and find forever homes.

This shelter won’t just be a place to keep animals — it will be a community effort to protect and care for lives that are too often overlooked. I believe that every animal matters, and with your help, we can prove it.

Donate to Make It Happen

Your donation helps make this shelter real. Every dollar goes toward food, medical care, shelter space, and rescue operations for animals in need. We’re building something from the ground up, and every bit of support brings us closer to opening the doors.

Our Goals

  • Secure a safe, warm building for rescued animals
  • Provide food, clean water, and medical care
  • Hire vets and volunteers to help with care
  • Create adoption programs to find loving homes
  • Educate the community on animal care and rescue

How You Can Help

Even if you can’t donate, there are so many ways to help. You can volunteer, foster an animal, spread the word, or donate supplies. If you’re interested in getting involved, we’d love to hear from you!
